An audio streaming app that allows users to listen to podcasts that are based on crime and mystery stories. Whether you need a bedtime story or just want to listen to something interesting, Earie's got you covered. Every day a new story is posted on the app, and even a quiz to test how much you know from the previous stories.

Flowchart

The first step in this project was creating a flowchart, which helps to envision the assessability, and how the user will move throughout the app.

Lo-Fidelity Wireframe

After creating the flow, I uses it to create a lo-fidelity wireframe of what the app will look like before gathering the digital assets.

Prototype

I used Adobe XD to design and prototype components and artboards that connects everything together. Within the app there are custom illustrations I created using procreate and Adobe Illustrator.
